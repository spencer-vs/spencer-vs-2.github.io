UEFA Champions League

Manchester United has won the UEFA Champions League three times, with their most famous victory coming in 1999 when they completed a dramatic comeback against Bayern Munich in the final. The club's first triumph in the competition was in 1968, making them the first English team to win the prestigious trophy, the won their third trophy in a thrilling final against fellow English side Chelsea that was decided by a penalty shootout. The club's success in the Champions League has solidified their status as one of the giants of European football, however recent years have seen a decline in their performance in the competition, with the club struggling to reach the latter stages.

UEFA Super Cup

Manchester United has won the UEFA Super Cup once, in 1991, when they defeated Red Star Belgrade 1-0 in the final. The match was played at the Olympic Stadium in Monaco, and the only goal of the game was scored by Brian McClair. This victory marked Manchester United's first European trophy under manager Sir Alex Ferguson and helped to establish the club as a force in European football. The UEFA Super Cup is contested between the winners of the UEFA Champions League and the UEFA Europa League, and while Manchester United has had success in both competitions, they have only managed to lift the Super Cup trophy once. The club's victory in 1991 remains a significant achievement in their history, showcasing their ability to compete at the highest level in European football.

UEFA Europa League

Manchester United has won the UEFA Europa League once, in 2017, when they defeated Ajax 2-0 in the final. The match was played at the Friends Arena in Stockholm, Sweden, and the goals were scored by Paul Pogba and Henrikh Mkhitaryan. This victory marked Manchester United's first triumph in the Europa League, having previously won the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup in 1991. The club's success in the Europa League added to their impressive collection of European trophies and showcased their ability to compete at a high level in European football. The Europa League is the second-tier club competition in European football, and Manchester United's victory in 2017 was a significant achievement for the club, especially considering the tragic events that occurred in Manchester just days before the final. The win provided a sense of unity and pride for the city and its football club, further solidifying Manchester United's status as one of the most successful clubs in European football history.

FA Cup

Manchester United has won the FA Cup 12 times, making them one of the most successful clubs in the history of the competition. The club's first FA Cup victory came in 1909, and their most recent triumph was in 2016 when they defeated Crystal Palace 2-1 in the final. The FA Cup is one of the oldest football competitions in the world, and Manchester United's success in the tournament has contributed to their rich history and tradition in English football. The club's victories in the FA Cup have included memorable moments, such as their famous comeback against Sheffield Wednesday in the 1990 final and their dramatic win over Millwall in 2004. The FA Cup remains a prestigious trophy for Manchester United, and they continue to compete fiercely for success in this historic competition.

EFL Cup

Manchester United has won the EFL Cup (also known as the League Cup) six times, with their most recent victory coming in 2022 when they defeated Newcastle United 2-0 in the final, becoming the first trophy of the Erik Ten Hag era, however it gave little more than false hope to the United faithful. The club's first triumph in the competition was in 1992, and they have enjoyed success in various editions of the tournament over the years. The EFL Cup is a knockout competition featuring clubs from the top four tiers of English football, and Manchester United's victories have included memorable moments, such as their dramatic win over Liverpool in the 2003 final. The EFL Cup remains an important trophy for Manchester United, and they continue to compete fiercely for success in this prestigious competition.
